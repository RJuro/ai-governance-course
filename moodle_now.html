Session Overview

Today we kick off your AI Governance Policy Brief Project. You will work in groups to research one of eight AI governance topics, write a policy brief for Denmark's Minister for Digitization, and present your findings to the class.

Today's Agenda

  • Introduction to ethical considerations surrounding data and AI
  • Overview of the project structure and timeline
  • Distribution of groups and group presentation topics
  • Initial discussion and research planning

Deliverables

  • Policy Brief (2,000-3,000 words): Written for Denmark's Minister for Digitization. Clear recommendations, evidence-based arguments, Danish and European context.
  • Presentation (15-20 minutes): Present to the class, defend your recommendations, engage with questions.

Group Size

Your policy briefs will be published on the course website after presentations, creating a resource for future students and anyone interested in Danish AI governance.
